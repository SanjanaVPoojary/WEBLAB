Announcement: Industrial Visit to Mysore & Madikeri

We are excited to announce that our college has organized an industrial visit to Mysore and Madikeri next week. This educational trip is aimed at providing hands-on industry exposure and a cultural experience for the students.

Day 2 Madikeri Industrial Exposure & Raja’s Seat Visit
Day 3 Return Group Feedback & Cultural Interaction
